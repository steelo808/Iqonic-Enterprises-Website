Frequently asked questions (FAQ)

credit repair

Q. When will I start to see the results on my credit report?

A. Results will start appearing within 30 to 90 days.

Q. What makes your company different from other credit repair companies?

A. Our company is different because we work to remove the inaccurate information while providing you with the tools and direction you need to rebuild and maintain your credit.

Q. Will our personal information be shared with anyone else?

A. No, your personal information is safe. The only time your information would be shared is if we are working with an affiliate partner who is involved in the dispute process.

Q. How do I get started?

A. Call us at or fill out the contact form on the website and somebody will get back to you within 24-48 hours.

Q. Can your service help me get ready to apply for a car loan?

A. Yes, by raising your credit score, you are more likely to qualify for a car loan.

Funding

Q. How long does it take to get funded?

A. On average, most clients receive funding within the first 30-45 days. For Performance Based clients, it’s 15-30 days.

Q. What if I don’t know my credit score?

A.We will complete an in-depth credit report for you and do a full review during your consultation.

Q. What if I have a bad credit score?

A. We work with you and coach you one-on-one to build up your credit report. Or you can use a credit partner that meets the requirements.
